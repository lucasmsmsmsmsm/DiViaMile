Cooking Class

Become the champion of dinner parties through this tailor-made cooking class!
Classes are taught by professional chefs, academics and experts of Italian and international renown.

Throughout the class, which can last from two to four hours, your chef will teach you a whole host of italian techniques from scratch,
incluiding a part dedicated to theoretical and practical lessons. At the end of this fully customizable private experience, you’ll have the time to linger
over a long lunch or dinner of the food you’ve made in class. Washed down, naturally, with a selection of fine wines, beers, and soft drinks.
If there is something in particular you would like to cook, you are welcome to specify with the chef beforehand.
