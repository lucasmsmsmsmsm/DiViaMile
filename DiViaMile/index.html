About Us

Located in the heart of Dublin city center, Di Via Mile is more than a traditional Italian restaurant. It is a place to travel with your taste buds, offering daily fresh homemade pastas. Since 1998, we've been passionate about our craft, working with local ingredients and offering a variety of Italian dishes complemented by an excellent wine selection. Twice awarded with three Michelin stars, we're proud to provide a cozy space for family and friends to share great food and memorable moments.

Our culinary school offers hands-on classes for cooking enthusiasts, taught by experienced chefs. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, we provide a welcoming environment to enhance your skills and passion for Italian cuisine.
